About Me

Hi, I'm Zac, a professional League of Legends player with a passion for playing Zac. With years of experience in the competitive scene, I've honed my skills to become one of the top Zac players in the world.

Formerly of Team Liquid and currently playing for 100 Thieves, my strategic playstyle and deep understanding of Zac's mechanics have led me to numerous victories and a dedicated fanbase. I specialize in turning team fights with perfectly timed ultimates and creating game-winning opportunities through my unique approach to the champion.

When I'm not playing professionally, I stream on Twitch to help aspiring players master Zac and share insights into the professional League of Legends scene. My goal is to elevate the perception of Zac as a competitive champion and inspire others to play creatively.
